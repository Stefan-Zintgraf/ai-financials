## MARKET REGIME FRAMEWORK (Four Quadrants)
1. **High Growth + High Inflation** = Overheating
2. **High Growth + Low Inflation** = Goldilocks
3. **Low Growth + High Inflation** = Stagflation
4. **Low Growth + Low Inflation** = Deflation/Recession

**Historical asset class performance in each regime**:
- **Goldilocks**: Equities > Credit > Commodities
- **Overheating**: Commodities > Cash > Inflation-linked bonds
- **Stagflation**: Commodities > Cash > Gold
- **Recession**: Long Bonds > Defensive Stocks > Cash
