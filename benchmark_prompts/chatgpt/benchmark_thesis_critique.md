# Benchmark Prompt: Critical Review of an Investment Thesis

## Role
You are reviewing an investment thesis written by a junior analyst.

---

## Investment Thesis (Excerpt)

> "We believe global equities will outperform bonds over the next decade because stocks always outperform in the long run. Interest rates are expected to fall, which is good for equities. Technological innovation will drive earnings growth. Therefore, portfolios should significantly increase equity exposure."

---

## Tasks

1. Critically evaluate the thesis.
2. Identify logical gaps, assumptions, and biases.
3. Rewrite the thesis into a professional version.
4. Suggest additional data to strengthen the argument.
5. Explain why weak investment narratives are dangerous.

**Instructions:**  
Be precise, constructive, and intellectually rigorous.
