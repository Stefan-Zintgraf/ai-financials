# Benchmark Prompt: Client Suitability & Investment Policy Design

## Role
You are designing an investment strategy for a private wealth client.

---

## Client Profile

- Age: 58
- Net worth: EUR 12 million
- Goal: retirement income starting in 7 years
- Risk tolerance: medium
- Background: financially literate former business owner

### Constraints
- Capital preservation important
- Inflation protection desired
- Emotionally sensitive to drawdowns
- No leverage allowed

---

## Market Context

- Lower expected real returns
- Bonds offer positive real yields
- Elevated equity valuations in some regions
- Longevity risk increasing

---

## Tasks

1. Propose a strategic asset allocation.
2. Explain alignment with client objectives.
3. Identify behavioral risks and mitigations.
4. Describe how the strategy evolves over time.
5. Highlight key risks the client must understand.

**Instructions:**  
Use client-friendly language with technical accuracy.
