# Benchmark Prompt: Stress Test & Scenario Analysis

## Role
You are tasked with performing a qualitative stress test on a diversified portfolio.

---

## Baseline Portfolio

- 60% Global Equities
- 30% Bonds (duration: 7 years)
- 10% Alternatives

- Expected return: 6.2% p.a.
- Expected volatility: 12.5%

---

## Scenarios

### Scenario A: Stagflation
- Inflation >4% for 3 years
- GDP growth <1%
- Rates remain elevated

### Scenario B: Hard Landing
- Deep recession (US & Europe)
- Equities fall 30%
- Credit spreads widen sharply

### Scenario C: Soft Landing + AI Boom
- Inflation normalizes quickly
- Productivity surge from AI
- Equity multiples expand

---

## Tasks

1. Assess portfolio performance under each scenario.
2. Identify shock absorbers vs. amplifiers.
3. Propose risk mitigation for the worst scenario.
4. Discuss robustness vs. return trade-offs.
5. Explain limitations of scenario analysis.

**Instructions:**  
Be concise but show clear financial reasoning.
